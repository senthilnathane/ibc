100% Annual Profit Distribution

IBC distributes 100% of annual net profit every year with 50% going to the founder and 50% to investors. No retention or ambiguity.

SAFE Investment Structure

Simple Agreement for Future Equity with a valuation cap of AED 3.8M and 20% discount. Equity upside is separate from dividend returns.
